Know Your Skin Type

Oily Skin

Skin becomes greasy quickly and is more prone to acne, clogged pores and excess shine.

Dry Skin

Skin feels tight, flaky or rough. Requires hydration and barrier repair products.

Combination Skin

Oily forehead and nose with dry cheeks. Needs balanced skincare products.

Sensitive Skin

Easily irritated by fragrance or harsh ingredients. Needs gentle and calming skincare.

Common Skincare Mistakes

  • Using too many active ingredients together
  • Skipping sunscreen indoors
  • Changing products too quickly
  • Over-exfoliating the skin
  • Not moisturizing oily skin
  • Sleeping with makeup or sunscreen on

Skincare FAQs

Can teenagers start skincare?

Yes. A simple routine with cleanser, moisturizer and sunscreen is enough for beginners.

Is sunscreen necessary indoors?

Yes, especially if sunlight enters through windows or you spend time near screens.

How long does skincare take to show results?

Most skincare products take around 4–8 weeks to show visible improvements.
