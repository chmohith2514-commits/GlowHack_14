Skincare FAQs

Can all skin types follow the same routine?

No, but basics like cleanser, moisturizer, and sunscreen are common for everyone.

When will skincare show results?

Usually 4–8 weeks depending on the product and skin type.

Is sunscreen necessary daily?

Yes, it protects against UV damage even indoors.
