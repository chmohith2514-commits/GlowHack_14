🧴 How Much Sunscreen Should You Apply?

Dermatologists recommend using the “two finger rule” for the face and neck. Reapply every 2–3 hours if you are outdoors.

Types Of Sunscreens

Gel Sunscreen

Best for oily and acne-prone skin. Lightweight and non-greasy.

Cream Sunscreen

Better for dry skin because it provides extra hydration and moisture.

Mineral Sunscreen

Gentle sunscreen suitable for sensitive skin. Usually contains zinc oxide.

Chemical Sunscreen

Lightweight sunscreen with minimal white cast. Popular for daily use.

Common Sunscreen Mistakes

  • Applying too little sunscreen
  • Skipping sunscreen on cloudy days
  • Not reapplying during outdoor activities
  • Using expired sunscreen products
  • Ignoring neck and ears while applying SPF

Sunscreen FAQs

Do I need sunscreen indoors?

Yes. UV rays can still enter through windows, especially during daytime.

What SPF is best for Indian weather?

SPF 50 is ideal for daily outdoor use in India, especially during summer.

Can oily skin skip sunscreen?

No. Oily skin still requires sun protection. Use lightweight gel sunscreens.
