Sunscreen FAQs

Do I need sunscreen indoors?

Yes. UV rays can still enter through windows and contribute to pigmentation and tanning.

How often should sunscreen be reapplied?

Ideally every 2–3 hours if you are outdoors or sweating frequently.

Can oily skin skip sunscreen?

No. Oily skin still needs UV protection. Gel sunscreens are usually more comfortable.
