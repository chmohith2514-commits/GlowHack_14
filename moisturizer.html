💡 When Should You Apply Moisturizer?

Apply moisturizer on slightly damp skin after washing your face to lock in hydration and protect the skin barrier.

Types Of Moisturizers

Gel Moisturizer

Lightweight and non-greasy. Best for oily and acne-prone skin.

Cream Moisturizer

Thick and deeply hydrating. Best for dry skin and winter weather.

Lotion Moisturizer

Balanced hydration suitable for combination and normal skin types.

Barrier Repair Moisturizer

Contains ceramides and calming ingredients to repair damaged skin barriers.

Common Moisturizer Mistakes

  • Skipping moisturizer because of oily skin
  • Using heavy creams in humid weather
  • Applying moisturizer on completely dry skin
  • Using fragranced moisturizers on sensitive skin
  • Changing moisturizers too frequently

Moisturizer FAQs

Do oily skin people need moisturizer?

Yes. Oily skin still needs hydration. Lightweight gel moisturizers work best.

Can moisturizer help damaged skin barrier?

Yes. Moisturizers with ceramides and hyaluronic acid help repair the skin barrier.

Should moisturizer be applied at night?

Yes. Nighttime moisturizing helps the skin repair and recover while sleeping.
